# Kamereo — Company Facts

## Snapshot
- **Full name:** KAMEREO INTERNATIONAL PTE. LTD. (Vietnam operating entity: KAMEREO)
- **What it is:** Vietnam's first tech-powered **B2B food sourcing platform** — connects restaurants, retailers, and institutions directly with fresh-produce suppliers and farmers.
- **Founded:** June 2018, Ho Chi Minh City.
- **Founders:**
  - **Taku Tanaka** — CEO & Co-founder. Japanese, ex-investment banker (Credit Suisse Securities Japan, equity sales for institutional investors, 2012–2014). Moved to Vietnam in 2015, joined **Pizza 4P's** as COO (finance, HR, procurement, Hanoi ops) — grew it from 1 store to 10 across 3 years (2015–2018). Left to found Kamereo in 2018.
  - **Hiroshi Tokaku** — Co-founder, technical/engineering background (was a lead software engineer at a major Japanese job-search engine).
- **Tagline / mission:** "Redefine the food business in Vietnam."

## Scale (most recent figures found)
- Revenue growth: **>300% average annual growth** since 2019 launch.
- **16,000+ SKUs** (2019 figure — likely higher today; not re-disclosed in later press).
- **3,000+ customers/clients** as of the Series B announcement (2025).
- Client roster spans HORECA (hotels/restaurants/cafes) to marquee names: **Starbucks, El Gaucho, Pizza 4P's**, plus supermarkets, convenience stores, factories, schools, hospitals.
- Two operating cities: Ho Chi Minh City + Hanoi (together >50% of Vietnam's GDP); daily refrigerated (cold-chain) transport connecting north-south; central-region expansion planned.
- Three temperature-zone warehousing (implies ambient / chilled / frozen zones).
- Widely described as **the largest player in Vietnam's B2B food supply e-commerce market.**

## Corporate structure / related entities
- **Kamereo** — core B2B platform (restaurants, retailers, institutions).
- **KameMart** — B2C spin-off, farm-to-table for Vietnamese households, same traceability/quality promise as the B2B side.
